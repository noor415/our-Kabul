Kabul Province Guide

Welcome to Kabul Province

Kabul is the capital and largest city of Afghanistan. It is the political, cultural, and economic center of the country.

Top Attractions in Kabul Province:

  • Shahr-e-Zahak (The Red City)
  • Babur's Gardens
  • National Museum of Afghanistan
  • Darul Aman Palace
  • Kabul City Mall

Interesting Facts about Kabul:

  • Kabul has a rich history dating back over 3,000 years.
  • It sits at an altitude of 1,800 meters (5,900 feet) above sea level.
  • The city is home to several universities and educational institutions.
  • Kabul is known for its vibrant markets and traditional Afghan crafts.
